Design in fusion 360 and InkScape

The Assignment was to design something in Fusion 360 and InkScape.

Fusion 360

Here I designed a case for the RaspBerry PI 3. The case is made with holes so that plugging in USB and other are easy. I also made an opening for the mini SSD, so that it can be installed without taking the RBP out of the case. The case is also designet with holes for screwing so that the RBS and cover won't fall of. The RaspBerry PI I did not design, but was fetched from GrabCad
